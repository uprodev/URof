Gastronomic performances

Book experience

Gastronomic performances are like contemporary works of art that combine different talents to tell a special story.

We bring together chefs, designers, artists, and musicians to create an experience meant to make viewers feel the touch of freedom deep inside, by using delicious food creations and beautiful visuals.

Night for
Freedom

Book experience

Immerse yourself in the extraordinary journey of empathy as we offer a unique opportunity for Latvian individuals to spend a night in a tent at a metro station.

This symbolic act sheds light on the plight of Ukrainian cities enduring missile attacks. Witness the strength and resilience of those impacted by the war.

Charity Mission

The charity's goal for this project is twofold: to facilitate the psychological rehabilitation of individuals affected by military engagements.

We aim to implement a comprehensive 10-day intensive program in the Ukrainian Carpathian Mountains focused on psychological rehabilitation, specifically tailored to support soldiers who have sustained injuries during combat.
