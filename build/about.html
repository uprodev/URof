Daria Kalashnikova

Focuses on capturing the essence of a scene or subject, often using loose brushwork.

"My whole life has been spent walking by the side of a bottomless chasm, jumping from stone to stone.”

These exhibitions provide an excellent platform for me to connect with fellow artists, art collectors, and art enthusiasts like you. I am looking forward to engaging in meaningful conversations about art, exchanging ideas, and learning from the diverse perspectives present at these events. I extend my heartfelt gratitude to everyone who has supported and encouraged me on my artistic journey.
